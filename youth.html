Kingdom Hoops

Each year, we work with youth from all over the state of Iowa. While we offer elite basketball training, our environment is one that creates integrative inclusion – a setting where diverse groups of youth come together, create relationships, and achieve goals together. Participants in our programs include boys and girls, Kindergarten through 11th grade. In 2015, over 360 youth from over 50 school districts in Iowa participated on Kingdom Hoops teams.
